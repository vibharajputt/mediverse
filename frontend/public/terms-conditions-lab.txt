MedAstraX Diagnostic Lab Terms and Conditions:

1. Lab Accreditation: You must maintain valid government accreditation and safety licensing for processing diagnostic tests.
2. Report Accuracy: All patient medical diagnostic reports and test results must be processed accurately and uploaded on time.
3. Patient Consent: Ensure patient privacy is respected and results are only shared with the patient and booking doctor.
4. Scheduled Bookings: Accept and process patient test appointments within your specified time slots.
5. Standard Hygiene: Maintain high standards of sanitary hygiene in lab collection centers.
